The thought of you.

I think of you all the time,
the time when I have some gain,
the time when there is so much of pain,
I think of you!
Your smile is so magical,
though it fills my eyes with tears.
but don't worry! there are no fears,
these are just words and I am no Shakespeare,
these are just vapours freshly condensed,
of the memories that hit me when I am tensed.
I miss you when I think of you!
And not when I don't!
So wake up and think- how many colors do I have,
to write in exam tomorrow or else I am dead,
Stretching myself to give my notebook another try,
reaching to the faith I failed long ago in the cry,
I reach to my bottle, to just let me once again fly,
Again I find it empty and completely dry.
I think of you all the time, I just don't know why?
