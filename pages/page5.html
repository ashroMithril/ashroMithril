Grow Like a Tree
I wish to grow like a tree,
With curves of it's own,
not all of them very perfect,
but a beauty that no one can frown,
with leaves and sometimes without,
it makes me wonder how it changes in a bout,
It takes time to settle in the roots,
in that period it dies just as many times as it shoots,
but once a stump is out,
then oh dear there is no doubt,
it grows higher and higher,
and becomes a life of itself.
Providing shade and shelter for free,
Giving humans and animals a pree,
By it’s own strength under the light of the sun,
Waving it’s pretty strands,
Along with all it’s friends,
