A part of me I am.

A part of me I was, a part of me I am
and a part of me I will be.
A whole but with lots of parts of myself, parts of thoughts, parts of
memories, parts of trouble, parts of love, parts of
mistakes, parts of achievements, parts of him,
parts of her, parts of you and parts of me.
A part of me can agree to drama of your taste,
a part of me will not as I eat from a different plate.
A part of me can forgive because humans are meant to make
mistakes but a part of me will forget that you existed
because apologies were way too late.
A part of me is shy as I take my own time,
A part of me is high, cause I love to drink wine.
A part of me senses my eyes wathcing you all dance,
Another part senses your eyes judging me at one glance.
A part of me can love, a part of me can hate,
Love the way you come close to me, hate the way
you say good bye!
A part of me is a poet, a part of me can't write,
A part of me dreams big, a part of me questions the same,
A part of me goes to class, a part of me stays back on the warm bed,
A part of me is home as well, a part of me with
the old friends,
A part of me running to the future, a part of me
fumbling back to the past,
A whole lot of me with lots of parts of myself.
I could be different if another part comes but all these
parts are in such a harmony, they never leave each other and
accepts new ones with all the heart.
But then again, only a part of me accepts it and a part of me
repels.
