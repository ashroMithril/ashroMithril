To every lover : Him and his.

I write when I am in pain, people soak the outburst of my happiness very well but only lonely writing can soak my tears.

If I ever made a promise, trust me I meant it!
I am not allowed to stay, but you will still see me standing there,
where we cherished the stars, where I felt alive for the first time,
you will see me hiding behind the door to peak a glance at your smile,
to see you riding into the sun, to see you crossing by me unknown,
To walk on the same streets as we once used to, trying to feel the warmth of your hands in mine,
To giggle on your PJs and then joining the echoes of silence again, just to be sane.
to think of you a thousand times, with tears rolling down,
a thunder to all the disaster.
Scared, shattered in my own belongings, I loved you but may be we were not supposed to be together,
You had no time and I had no patience.
We all have ambitions and it was malignant to trust but benign to dare,
And now, a part of me will always be with you and a part of you will always be with me.
For the one who I loved more than I loved myself.
